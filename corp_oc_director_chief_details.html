Ahmad Abdulaziz A A Al-Neama

Ahmad Abdulaziz A A Al-Neama was appointed as the President Director and Chief Executive Officer of Indosat Ooredoo at the Extraordinary General Shareholders Meeting (EGMS) dated 1 August 2019

Experience
  1. He has been 15 years experienced with Ooredoo Group since 2004 in various operational and management roles, with a proven track record of effective management, team leadership, project management, business development and strategy. Group Chief Technology Officer at Ooredoo Group (2017 - 2019)
  2. Chief Sales & Service Officer Ooredoo Qatar (2015 - 2017)
  3. Board of Commissioners at Indosat Ooredoo (2018 - 2019)
  4. Board member of Ooredoo Oman, Ooredoo Myanmar and Ooredoo Tunisia (2017 - 2019)
  5. Board of Directors Intaleq Qatar (2019 - present)
Education
  1. He earned a degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from the University of Colorado, Denver, USA (2002-2003).
